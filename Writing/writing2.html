One human-made system that brings out the best in me is capitalism. It’s not an ideology I necessarily romanticize, but as a system that functions as a constant catalyst pushing me to work harder, think more creatively, and take ownership of my own growth. The promise that effort can translate into tangible reward motivates me in a way that few other forces do. Knowing that the benefits money can bring are out there and accessible if I push myself enough prompts a kind of disciplined ambition in me. It forces me to confront my limits, develop new skills, and refuse to be complacent. In that sense, capitalism as a system empowers me to express my best qualities not because it hands me anything, but because it doesn't.
