Offering a new vision for the modern digital landscape, “calm technology” stood out to me as a compelling concept. In contrast to today’s overwhelming environment filled with notifications, constant connectivity, and clickbait thumbnails, the reading suggests that the future of good design should actually recede into the background. It explains an alternative setting where technology informs us without demanding our full attention, operating in the “periphery” of our awareness unless needed. This idea challenges the dominant model of engagement driven design that is prevalent in modern days, where apps and platforms compete aggressively for user attention.
